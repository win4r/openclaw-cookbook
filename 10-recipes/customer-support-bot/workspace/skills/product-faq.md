## Common Issues

**Q: I cannot log in.**
A: Try these steps in order:
1. Verify you are using the correct email address
2. Reset your password at [URL]
3. Clear your browser cache and cookies
4. Try a different browser or incognito/private mode
5. If none of these work, contact us at [email]

**Q: The app is slow or not loading.**
A: Try these steps:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try a different browser or device
3. Clear your browser cache
4. Disable browser extensions (some ad blockers interfere)
5. Check our status page at [status URL] for any ongoing incidents

**Q: I am seeing an error message.**
A: Please share the exact error message and I will help troubleshoot. If possible, also tell me:
- What you were trying to do when the error appeared
- What browser and device you are using
- Whether this is the first time or a recurring issue
